# 🧘 Panchakarma Management System

## 📌 Description
A web-based Panchakarma Management System developed to manage Ayurvedic clinic operations such as patient records, therapy scheduling, treatment tracking, and billing.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Digitize patient and treatment records  
- Simplify appointment scheduling  
- Improve clinic workflow efficiency  
- Maintain accurate billing records  

## 🚀 Features
- Patient Registration & Management  
- Appointment Scheduling  
- Treatment & Therapy Tracking  
- Panchakarma Procedure Management  
- Doctor & Staff Management  
- Billing & Payment System
